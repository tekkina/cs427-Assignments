my favorite dish is "Doro wote"

"doro wot" is a chicken stew, a very spicy Ethiopian cultural food. we need the following ingriedents to prepare "doro wot"

  1. chicken meat
  2. powder red paper
  3. a kilo of onion
  4. ginger
  5. garlic
